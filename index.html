The Journey at a Glance

These documents walk through the next evolution of the platform—from where we are today, through the mechanics of migration, to the operational model that keeps everything running smoothly. No more big-bang releases. No more dependency inconsistencies. No more weird UI flicker issues. Every domain deploys independently with canary/blue-green rollouts, shift-left quality gates, and automated CVE scanning.

Full evolution journey: five stages from monolith to governed domain-driven shells
Industry-Standard Pattern Every phase in this migration uses battle-tested patterns: Strangler Fig decomposition, Domain-Driven Design bounded contexts, W3C Web Components, and Backend-for-Frontend isolation. No experimental technology. No vendor lock-in.
